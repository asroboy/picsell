About JadeScape

JadeScape is located within Bishan-Thomson area. It is within strolling distance to Marymount MRT (Circle Line). It is set to be the tallest development in the area. Nested with abundance of amenities, it is expected to be well received by many.

Catholic High Primary is located within 1km from the project. Shunfu Mart with the popular food centre and the market is just located opposite of the project. Shengsiong supermarket is also within 5 minutes walking distance.

Connectivity…
Connectivity in the area is also very accessible with Marymount MRT (Circle Line) less than 200 metres away. Bishan MRT (North-South and Circle Line Interchange) is just a stop away.

The upcoming Upper Thomson MRT (Thomson-East Coast Line) is also about 600 metres away.

It is just 5 MRT stops from Orchard and 10 stops from Raffles Place. Public buses are also available along Marymount Road and Shunfu Road.

Accessibility…
The accessibility to major expressways like Pan Island Expressway (PIE) and Central Expressway (CTE) is a short distance away. This will make driving to other parts of Singapore in quicker time and more convenient.
